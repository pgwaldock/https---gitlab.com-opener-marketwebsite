Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Kontainers?

Kontainers is a company created based on need. The process of ocean freight forwarding is burdened with inefficiency, lack of upfront options and hidden charges.

We aim to solve this problem by leveraging technology and good honest customer service.Our platform instantly gives you rates for over 250 of the largest ports for UK exporters (and growing). It also gives you the ability to book and transact your shipment online in seconds from your door, including customs, where real-time updates drastically cut time spent looking at shipping and increase productivity and service to your client.

2. Who we are and what is our strategy?

At Kontainers, we have over 50 years of experience ranging from freight forwarding, technology, customer service and business development. We retain customers by providing the largest amount of options possible, being totally transparent in our pricing and the best customer service experience this industry has ever seen.

3. What if my destination is missing?

No problem, we are adding more daily but mail us on help@kontainers.co.uk and we will prioritise them for you.

4. How secure is Kontainers?

Our site uses world class 128bit encryption technologies used by online banks. Your payment details never reach our servers, they go directly to stripe a specialised payment gateway (similar to paypal).

5. Is it really that simple to book a container in under one minute?

Yes, but not only that, after your work is done sit back and watch the shipment update in real-time.

6. What if I need help? Or have questions about what we do?

Call us from 6am to 8pm GMT, drop us an email or use the instant chat feature on the bottom right of your screen.
