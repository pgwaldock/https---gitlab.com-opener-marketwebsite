How it works

Kontainers removes the hassle of multiple phone calls, faxes for shipping and updating of your containers. By providing rates and schedules up front with no hidden costs we are the most transparent in the industry and aim to give our customers the most options possible.

STEP 1

Start a new quote from our home page using the Get Instant Quotes button and we'll guide you through the process.

STEP 2

With Kontainers, getting your FCL and LCL freight shipping quotes could not be easier.

Our user-friendly interface removes the complexity so we can present you with instant quotes in a few clicks.

STEP 3

Choose the sailing that suits your requirements best. Whether it's departure date, transit time or price.

Our rates are competitive but if you usually pay less for your selected sailing you can use our Make an Offer feature and we'll try and negotiate a better rate for you.

STEP 4

Fill in the order. If you entered your trucking and customs requirements we can also handle that for you.

Trucking

Customs

STEP 5

Review your order and confirm the cost. You can edit at any time up to sailing.

STEP 6

You can now opt for one of 3 methods of payment. Which ever option you pick, payment will not get processed until the shipment is on board.

STEP 7

From here your shipment is done. Sit back and watch real-time updates on your dashboard. Once on board it will also include bill of lading preview and once you tick ok it will be issued.

STEP 8

For your next order just copy the last order so it only takes seconds!
